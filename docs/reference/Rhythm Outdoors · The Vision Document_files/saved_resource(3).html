Safety & Hospitality

Horseshoe Bay Sporting Club operates a strict Cold Range policy: once a guest is served an alcoholic beverage, their range privileges conclude for the evening. Shooting and beverage service are sequenced — never combined. This is the foundation of our hospitality, not an afterthought to it.

All shooting activities are conducted under the direct supervision of nationally certified instructors at a maximum 1:5 instructor-to-student ratio. Equipment, ammunition, eye and ear protection, and a thorough range safety briefing are included for every guest who chooses to shoot.
