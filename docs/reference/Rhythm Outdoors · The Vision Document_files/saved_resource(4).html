What to Wear

Hill Country sun, outdoor range, active afternoon. Dress accordingly.

Long Pants
Jeans, chinos, or technical pants. No shorts on the range.
Closed-Toe Shoes
Boots, sneakers, or trail shoes. No sandals or flip-flops.
Long-Sleeve or Full Shirt
Breathable fabric recommended. No tank tops or open shoulders — for protection from sun and ejected casings.
Ball Cap
Strongly recommended. Brim catches casings and shades the eyes from afternoon glare.
Layers
May afternoons in the Hill Country can swing 20°. A light jacket or vest is rarely a mistake.
Provided On Site Sunscreen, eye protection, hearing protection, hydration. If your team prefers their own brand or SPF, by all means.
