# Helice / per-target pricing — client summary

You flagged that **Helice** needs a pricing style the catalog didn't support — priced
**per target (bird)** rather than per person. That's now built, and it's general enough to cover
**any future per-target game**, not just Helice.

## What you can do now

**A new "Per-target" pricing type** is available when you create or edit an experience in the
dashboard — alongside the existing Guest fee / Lesson / Class / Quote options. Pick it, and you set:

- **Member rate per target** and **Public (non-member) rate per target** — e.g. $2.50 / $2.95.
- **Allotment size** — the block targets are sold in (e.g. 30 → guests choose 30 / 60 / 90…).
- **Maximum targets** *(optional)* — a cap on how many a guest can pick. Leave it blank for no limit.
- **Unit label** — what you call a target (defaults to "target"; could be "bird", etc.).

**A reusable setup/session fee** — also built into every experience (not just Helice):

- A flat **fee charged once per outing to everyone** (members and guests), e.g.
  **$49.50 "Setup / ring fee."**
- You can add an **optional description** explaining what the fee is for (e.g. "We staff the ring
  every session"). Leave the amount blank for no fee.

## What guests see

When someone builds an estimate and picks a per-target experience, they get a **+/− stepper** that
moves in your allotment size (30s), starting at one block, and **stops at your maximum** with a small
note like *"max 300 targets."* The live price updates as they go and shows the target cost, the setup
fee, and — for non-member guests — the standard guest fee on top. **Members shoot on dues**, so no
guest fee for them. Ammo stays a separate add-on, as before.

The price they see flows straight through to the **bid** your team finishes — guest preview and staff
bid always match.

## Things you can adjust yourself anytime

Rates, allotment size, the maximum, the setup fee and its description — all editable per club in the
dashboard, no developer needed.
