## How to use this in a meeting

1. Before the meeting — skim `docs/5-14-26-client-questions.md` and the latest dated folder under `plan/questions/`.
2. During the meeting — work through the questions in conversational order; jot answers in the moment.
3. After the meeting — for each answered question:
   - Update its file's `Status:` line and fill in the `## Answer` block.
   - Move a one-line entry to the "Resolved" section below with the date.
   - If the answer was in `docs/need_answers.md`, strike through the original there too so the canonical foundational list stays current.
