# Q2 — Display name in the From header

**Category:** Email configuration
**Status:** Open · surfaced 2026-05-30

## The question

Should every email say "Rhythm Outdoors", or should it switch per property?

- **"Rhythm Outdoors" for everything** — simpler; the property is named in the email body anyway (recommended)
- Branded per property (e.g. "Horseshoe Bay Sporting Club <bookings@…>", "Hog Heaven Sporting Club <bookings@…>")

## Recommendation

Single "Rhythm Outdoors". The customer just went through that property's booking
flow — they know which property they booked.
