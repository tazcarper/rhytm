# Q15 — Pre-event email cadence

**Category:** Workflow design
**Status:** Open · surfaced 2026-05-30

Two sub-questions. These fire once a booking is confirmed (signed + deposit paid).

## 15a — Is this cadence right?

- **T-14 days** — gear list, directions, what to expect
- **T-3 days** — reminder, weather, parking
- **T-1 day** — final confirmation, who to ask for, arrival time
- **T+1 day** — post-event follow-up

**Recommendation:** this sequence as-is. Easy to adjust per booking type later
if needed (e.g. shorter cadence for half-day visits).

## 15b — Membership CTA in the post-event email (public guests only)?

The day after a great experience is the highest-leverage moment to invite a
public guest to apply for membership. Should the T+1 email include a direct link
to the application?

- **Yes, include the membership CTA** (recommended)
- No — keep post-event purely thank-you (no upsell)

**Recommendation:** Include it, but as a soft secondary CTA below the thank-you,
not the lead.
