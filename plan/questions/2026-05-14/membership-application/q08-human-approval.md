**Category:** Membership Application
**Status:** Open · surfaced 2026-05-14 · restated in the 2026-05-30 packet
**Blocks:** Inngest membership workflow design · admin UI approval action · member portal invite timing

## Context

The system can either (A) automatically grant membership the moment payment
clears, or (B) keep a human approval step where the team reviews the application
and clicks approve before membership is granted. Option B is recommended —
sporting club membership is relationship-driven and selective; automating
approval fully feels off-brand. The paperwork burden is eliminated either way;
the question is whether the team retains the final yes.

## The question

- Should membership require manual team approval after the application and payment are submitted, or should payment alone trigger membership being granted?
