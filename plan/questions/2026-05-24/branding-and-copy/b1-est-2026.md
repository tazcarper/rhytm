# B1 — "Est. 2026" on the home page

**Category:** Branding & customer-facing copy
**Status:** Open · surfaced 2026-05-24 · low priority but easy to fix
**Blocks:** Final home page copy review
**Source:** `app/page.tsx:28` — `<span className={s.heroEstablished}>Est. 2026</span>`

## The question

The home page hero currently shows "Est. 2026". Is that accurate? Three possibilities:

- **It's correct** — the Rhythm Outdoors umbrella brand was actually founded in 2026. Leave as-is.
- **It should reflect the oldest property** — if HSB / Hog Heaven / Packsaddle has been operating since (say) 2008, the umbrella brand should probably claim that heritage too. Change to "Est. 2008" or similar.
- **Remove the line entirely** — if there's no clean answer ("technically Rhythm Outdoors LLC was incorporated last year but the properties go back decades"), drop the badge rather than mislead.

## Why it matters

"Est. 2026" reads as a brand-new startup to a first-time visitor. That's fine if accurate — but if HSB has decades of operating history, the umbrella claim shouldn't undersell the heritage. Brand credibility lives on the front door.

## What it unblocks

A one-line code change in `app/page.tsx`. Trivial; just need the right year (or the decision to drop it).

## Recommendation

If even one of the three properties has been operating more than 5 years, lean toward "Est. \<oldest-property-year\>" with brief footer copy clarifying the umbrella was formed in 2026. Otherwise leave as-is.
